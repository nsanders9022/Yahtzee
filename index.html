How to Play

To begin the game press on the green "roll dice" button.

Click on the image of the dice you want to hold.

After clicking "roll dice" again all dice that have not been held will be rolled.

The dots on the "roll dice" button signify how many more rolls are left before you must submit your points

Rules

Game Summary

The object of Yahtzee is to obtain the highest score from throwing 5 dice.

The game consists of 14 rounds. On each turn, roll the dice up to 3 times to get the highest scoring combination for one of 14 categories. The score is determined by a different rule for each category.

After you finish rolling, you must submit a score or a zero in one of the categories.

Game Play

To start with, roll all the dice. After rolling you can either score the current roll (see below), or re-roll any or all of the dice.

You may only roll the dice a total of 3 times. After rolling 3 times you must choose a category to score.

You may score the dice at any point in the round, i.e. it doesn't have to be after the 3rd roll.

Ending a Game

The game ends once all 14 categories have been scored.
